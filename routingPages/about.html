ABOUT ELITEBODY STUDIO

Whether you’re a first-time gym user or a veteran member, we’re here to provide a workout environment in which anyone - and everyone - can be comfortable.

EliteBody Studio is a premium fitness centre that offers a wide range of fitness services. We are dedicated to providing the best fitness experience to our members. Our fitness centre is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and our trainers are highly experienced. We offer a wide range of fitness classes and personal training sessions. Our fitness centre is open 24/7 and we have a dedicated team of staff to ensure that our members have a great experience. We also have a juice bar and a sauna for our members to relax after a workout. We are committed to providing the best fitness experience to our members and we are constantly working to improve our services. Looking for a little extra guidance or inspiration to help you meet your fitness goals? As a PF member, you can take advantage of our free fitness training* or find your motivation by reading real member experiences on Planet of Triumphs. It’s our goal to provide a clean, safe, welcoming environment for anyone who walks through our door, and all the equipment, amenities and support you need once you’re here.
